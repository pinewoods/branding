About us

We are a brazilian company for developing Internet of Things technologies for manufactories, agriculture and small business.

Our background is cloud-based software development, connecting sensors, machines and platforms over Internet.

We believe that Education is the first step to build innovative ideas. We are passionated about share our knowledge and experience.

Mission & Core Values

We believe that eficiency makes the world better. Using IoT devices to collect and analyze data, it's possible to create smart machines, which not only perceive and interact with its enviroment, but also communicate with people and other machines.

Our core values relies in people as the most important and valuable component in this endeavor to breakthrough the way how the information and companies work.

  • Respect
  • Integrity
  • Effiency
  • Hard work
  • Commitment
